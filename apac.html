APAC

University of Wollongong

Location: Wollongong, Australia

Program Description: Since 1960, the University of Wollongong has been committed to creating an enjoyable and productive experience for study abroad students. The University of Wollongong, with its enrollment of 12,000 students, is a modern learning center with a reputation for high-quality teaching and research. Currently, 2,000 international students are part of the student body, coming from all over the world.
