University of Wollongong

Location: Wollongong, Australia

Program Description: Since 1960, the University of Wollongong has been committed to creating an enjoyable and productive experience for study abroad students. The University of Wollongong, with its enrollment of 12,000 students, is a modern learning center with a reputation for high-quality teaching and research. Currently, 2,000 international students are part of the student body, coming from all over the world.

Program duration: Semester

Estimated cost: $34,640.00

University of Canterbury

Location: Christchurch, New Zealand

Program Description: The University of Canterbury is New Zealand’s second oldest university and is known for its innovative courses, student support, and prestigious learning. The university has five ‘colleges’: Arts (Humanities and Social Sciences), Business and Law, Engineering, Education Health and Human Development, and Science. Canterbury is a public university with a total enrollment of over 16,000 students. Students and faculty at Canterbury represent a variety of countries, making the university a diverse and inspiring learning environment.

Program duration: Semester

Estimated cost: $19,997.00

Learn more on the UNC Study Abroad Website


Murdoch University

Location: Perth, Australia

Program Description: Established in 1973, Murdoch is committed to excellence in research and teaching. It has an extensive range of international contacts, resulting in a cosmopolitan campus with over 3,000 international students in a student body of 25,000. The campus itself makes Murdoch an excellent place to study as well: the university is set in natural bushland and is landscaped with native Australian flora. The academic year at Murdoch is divided into two semesters: the first semester runs from February to June, and the second semester runs from July to November. Students should keep in mind that the Australian summer is from December to February and the winter is from June to August. Year-long students may attend either the US academic calendar (July-June, with a long break between terms) or the Australian calendar year (February-December).

Program duration: Semester

Estimated cost: $30,490.00

Learn more on the UNC Study Abroad Website
